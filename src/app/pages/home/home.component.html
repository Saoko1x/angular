Exploring the World of Web Development at 21

As a 21-year-old developer from Mexico, I'm constantly amazed by the ever-evolving landscape of web technologies. From React to Vue, the frontend world is a playground of possibilities.

Did you know that JavaScript was created in just 10 days? Yet, it's become the backbone of modern web development. The rapid growth of frameworks like Next.js and Remix is reshaping how we build web applications.

The Rise of JAMstack and Static Site Generators

JAMstack architecture is revolutionizing web performance and security. Tools like Gatsby and Astro are making it easier than ever to create lightning-fast websites. As a young developer, it's exciting to be part of this transformation.

Mexico's tech scene is booming, with a 22% growth in the IT sector in 2021. We're not just consuming technology; we're actively contributing to its global development.

The Future is Serverless

Serverless computing is changing the game for backend development. With services like AWS Lambda and Azure Functions, we can focus more on code and less on infrastructure. It's an exciting time to be a full-stack developer!

Web accessibility is more important than ever. Did you know that over 1 billion people worldwide have some form of disability? As developers, we have the power to make the web inclusive for everyone.

The world of web development is vast and full of opportunities. At 21, I'm just getting started, and I can't wait to see what the future holds!
